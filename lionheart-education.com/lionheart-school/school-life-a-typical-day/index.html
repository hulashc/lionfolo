Lionheart School

Our School Life & Approach

Lionheart School delivers an exceptionally bespoke model of one-to-one education, built for students who need stability, stretch and specialist understanding. Every day is shaped around the learner, ensuring academic continuity, emotional wellbeing and clear progress towards future placements.

Our Approach

What to expect at our school

At Lionheart School, education is never one-size-fits-all. We create individualised programmes that empower students, giving them the tools to become self-sufficient, confident and ambitious learners.

  • One-to-one emphasis

    This will probably be the hardest your child will ever be required to work. The one-to-one lesson format ensures we keep the focus and attention of your child.

    We know this is intensive and exhausting and so we ensure the majority of studying is done within the school.

    The result is rapid progress, deeper understanding, and renewed confidence.

    In addition, a curriculum manager always oversees a student’s daily programme, ensuring attainment and accountability.

  • The best tutors in the field

    Our tutors bring not only subject expertise but also specialist knowledge.
    Whether that expertise lies in learning differences, school entrance preparation, or supporting complex educational journeys, every child is matched with someone who understands their needs and can help them move forward with confidence.

  • Expert consultancy & onwards transition

    We combine tuition with strategic placement advice, helping families secure places at leading day and boarding schools, sixth forms, and universities.

    Our goal is simple: that every student leaves Lionheart stronger, more assured, and aware of the potential they carry.

A Typical Day at Lionheart School

Our in-house chef serves a delicious breakfast for all the students whilst they read newspapers and magazines to keep up to date on news and world events. This is important for general knowledge and also for interviews at future schools.

This time is divided into three one-hour lessons with breaks. They are multisensory, interactive, fun and move precisely at the pace of the individual student at that time and on that day. Due to this, the speed of progress can be astonishing.

Lunch. This is a choice of freshly prepared dishes utilising the highest quality organic ingredients. The chef consults with the students to include their favourite dishes in the weekly menu whilst maintaining a balanced and nutritional diet.

Lessons and private study sessions continue into the afternoon. Always bespoke, always one-to-one. A healthy afternoon snack is baked and served by our chef in the afternoon break to ensure that our students stay energised and are eager to learn.

LSL remains open into the evening for further lessons and private study sessions if needed and to allow the students to complete their homework in school, leaving their evenings free to relax at home and prepare for the following day.

Not your average school meal…

After training with the Leith’s School of Food and Wine and London Cookery school, Georgia started running her own catering business. Students have their favourites — from homemade sourdough pizza and fresh pasta to warming curries and vibrant seasonal salads!

Georgia takes pride in creating food that’s both nourishing and comforting, with plenty of variety to suit different tastes and dietary needs. Her approach ensures that mealtimes aren’t just about eating well, but feeling well, too.
